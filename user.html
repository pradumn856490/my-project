About Rohit Furniture

Welcome to Rohit Furniture, where we turn your house into a home with our exquisite collection of premium wooden furniture. Established with a passion for craftsmanship, we specialize in creating durable, stylish, and functional pieces that last for generations.

Our Specialties:

  • Quality Timber: We use 100% seasoned teak and rosewood for maximum durability.
  • Custom Designs: Every home is unique. We provide tailor-made furniture that fits your specific space.
  • Modern Aesthetics: Our designs blend traditional hand-carving with modern minimalist styles.

Why Choose Us?

At Nashik's premier furniture showroom, we believe in "Quality over Quantity". From modular kitchens to handcrafted doors and luxury wardrobes, our expert artisans ensure every piece undergoes a rigorous quality check before reaching your doorstep.

Located in the heart of Nashik, we are dedicated to providing the best furniture solutions for homes, offices, and commercial spaces.
